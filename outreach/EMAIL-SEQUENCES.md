# Outreach Sequences — emails, DMs, follow-ups

Rules that make these work:
- **One named person.** Never `info@`.
- **The link is the pitch.** Get them to *play*, don't make them read.
- **Personalize line 1.** One specific, true reason you're emailing *them* (a game they
  just launched, a crash title in their catalogue, a program they run).
- **Short.** Under 120 words. One ask.
- **Warm the demo first** so it's not cold-starting when they click.
- Replace `[Aggregator]`, `[First]`, `[specific hook]`. Signature: **team@clutchstudios.co**.
