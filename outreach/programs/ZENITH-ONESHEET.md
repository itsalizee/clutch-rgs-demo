# Zenith — Flagship One-Sheet

**A shared-round vertical climb. One climber, everyone in the round, secure before the fall.**

▶ Play: https://clutch-rgs-demo.onrender.com/zenith/  ·  Verify fairness: /fairness

---

## The hook
Everyone in the round watches **one climber** ascend a neon spire in real time. **Altitude is
the multiplier.** You have one decision — **secure your winnings before the climber falls.**
The higher it climbs, the bigger the payout and the greater the tension, shared by the whole
room at once.

## Why it isn't "just another crash clone"
- **Same proven, addictive core** (a shared rising multiplier + a single cash-out decision) —
  but a **completely fresh identity**: a 3D neon *ascent*, not a plane on a curve. It escapes
  crash's visual saturation while keeping crash's stickiness.
- **Every evidence-based engagement lever, stacked:**
  - a **live shared "now"** the whole round experiences together (the social/spectator layer)
  - a single **cash-out decision** → illusion of control (feels skill-based on a chance outcome)
  - **fast round cadence** → the adrenaline loop
  - **clip-ready micro-episodes** (climb → tension → the fall) — the "reaction engine" that
    spreads on short-form and streams

## The maths
| | |
|---|---|
| **Format** | Crash-family · shared multiplayer rounds · vertical climb |
| **RTP** | Operator-configurable (demo 97%) — configured RTP == realized RTP, harness-proven |
| **Volatility** | Very high — median low, long right tail (the big-climb dopamine) |
| **Round** | Betting window → shared climb → single cash-out → reveal · fast cadence |
| **Fairness** | Commit-reveal over a pre-committed server-seed chain + player entropy + public block-hash — player-verifiable every round |

## Retention & social loop
Live all-bets feed · round history · auto-cashout · big-climb moments built to be clipped and
shared · tournaments/leaderboards-ready. (No fabricated/bot players — real round participants
only.)

## Integration
Ships on the same **single REST + WebSocket + seamless-wallet** contract as our whole
catalogue. Server-authoritative; the client is a thin renderer. Drop-in iframe or SDK, web &
mobile.
