# Clutch Studios — Emerging-Studio Program Application Kit

The research verdict was clear: for a brand-new studio, **distribution comes through an
emerging-studio program**, not solo certification + cold outreach. Two are confirmed real
and a fit for us:

- **Relax Gaming — Silver Bullet** — onboards growing studios onto Relax's RGS + operator
  network (50+ partner studios → 250+ operators). *Primary source verified.*
- **Pariplay — Ignite** — emerging-studio program; studios build on Pariplay's
  already-certified platform + Fusion distribution (recently onboarded 916 Gaming, a US
  emerging studio). *Verified.*

**Why these matter for us specifically:** they provide the two things we don't have —
**certification (via their platform) and distribution.** We bring a differentiated
flagship + a working provably-fair RGS. That's the trade.

## The 6-step application playbook
1. **Fill the placeholders** — everywhere you see `[BRACKETS]` (founder name/bio, entity,
   jurisdiction, contact). Do **not** invent traction, team, cert, or awards.
2. **Warm the demo link** and confirm all six games load.
3. **Find the contact** — Relax: partnerships/Silver Bullet via relax-gaming.com/partners;
   Pariplay: Ignite via pariplayltd.com. Also catch them at ICE/SiGMA/SBC.
4. **Send the cover email** (in each program file) with the demo link + a 1-page PDF of
   `STUDIO-DECK.md` and `ZENITH-ONESHEET.md`.
5. **On the call:** have them *play Zenith*, then walk the fairness page and the "one
   integration, built-to-certify" story. Use `READINESS-QA.md`.
6. **Honesty is the strategy** — we're new; the product and the tech are the proof. Never
   overstate cert/license/traction.
