## 5 — The technology (the real asset)
A production **server-authoritative RGS** (Node/TypeScript):
- **Outcomes, balances, timing all server-side** — the client is a thin renderer (the #1
  certification requirement).
- **Provably fair** — pre-committed server-seed hash chain + player entropy + public
  block-hash (crash); commit-reveal shuffles (mines/climb). Every round player-verifiable.
- **Seamless wallet** — idempotent debit / credit / rollback by txId, integer minor units,
  auto-reconciliation. A per-aggregator adapter maps to *your* wallet API.
- **Built to certify** — deterministic replay, append-only audit logs, an RTP harness. The
  GLI-19 technical pack is largely in place.
